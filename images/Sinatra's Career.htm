Frank Sinatra

Frank Sinatra also known as the Chairman of the Board was a generational singer. This leader of the Ratpac made a great impact on the culture of music and will remain in the minds of people forever. In addition to his talents in singing he also won awards for his acting.

  • The Golden Globes Cecil B Demille for Lifetime Achievement in 1971
